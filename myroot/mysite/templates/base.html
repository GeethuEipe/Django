HR Heads from corporates say that students have the necessary theoretical skills, but when it comes to the practical part, they lack big time. Lately, we have observed that in our country ‘college life’ has become synonymous with ‘fun’ and ‘fests’. Ask any student and they’ll tell you how strongly they believe that college is meant for having the time of your life with friends. With such a mentality being accepted and promoted, there is no doubt that employability will be decreasing among the Indian workforce. We need universities to introduce the concept of compulsory internships and practical skill learning.
